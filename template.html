About ISI LAB

Introduction to ISI Lab

Intelligent Service Integration(ISI) Lab at KAIST develops technology for Indoor-and-outdoor integrated GPS system(I-GPS). Sensor fusion positioning, WiFi positioning, Pedestrian Dead Reckoning (PDR), AI-based landmark detection, Smartphone-based sensor fusion positioning, Autonomous robot for sensing data collection are active research areas in ISI Lab. The eventual research goal of ISI Lab is to develop an I-GPS system that can provide positioning service not only outdoor environment but also in any building regardless of the presence of indoor layouts and wireless signals. ISI Lab is also developing I-GPS HW modules that can integrate various sensors and signals such as inertial sensor, barometer, magnetometer, and light sensors. ISI Lab has developed an I-GPS service platform, KAILOS, on which anyone can implement indoor positioning service for any building globally. Indoor-and-outdoor integrated location tags have been developed by integrating KAILOS with the I-GPS HW modules. Metaverse and Non-Fungible Token(NFT) is another research direction of ISI Lab. ISI Lab’s research area in this direction includes AI-based text to image creation, card-based NFT contents registration and distribution, NFT security, and AR photo machine. The following is the current projects and research outcomes of ISI Lab.
